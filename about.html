Our Story

Named after Pakistan's national animal — built for its next generation of builders

The markhor climbs terrain most animals avoid, using nothing but grip, patience, and precision. Cute Markhor Store was built on the same idea: give Pakistani developers, students, and makers reliable access to the hardware they need, without the friction of unreliable sellers or confusing checkouts.

We started with one board — the ESP32 — because it's the one developers ask for most. Every unit we ship is tested, every order is confirmed by a real person, and every payment method is one Pakistani developers already use daily.

Our Mission

Make trustworthy developer hardware available to every builder in Pakistan — with clear pricing, honest stock, and fast dispatch.

Our Vision

To become the go-to store for IoT and embedded tools across South Asia, known for reliability as much as product quality.

Why Choose Us

Genuine components, local payment methods, real WhatsApp support, and a checkout built to be fast on any phone.
